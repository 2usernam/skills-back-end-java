Intro to Spring Boot: Controllers

You may see a pop-up in the lower left-hand corner in your Event Log that reads: Unindexed remote maven repositories found.

  1. Select Open repositories list. This will open the Preferences window to Build, Execution, Deployment > Build Tools > Maven > Repositories .
  2. Select the Maven repository (https://repo1.maven.org/maven2) and click Update on the left-hand side.

This update usually takes about 20 minutes but can last up to an hour.
